Chania September 2026

Workshop on Model Theory and Applications
Chania, August 30 - September 5, 2026

     

Organizers: Pantelis Eleftheriou (Leeds), Kobi Peterzil (Haifa)

The goal of this workshop is to bring together researchers in model theory, with interests around o-minimality, NIP expansions, combinatorics, and graph theory. We plan to run the workshop in an Oberwlfach-style format, where all participants will be considered for giving talks and the program will be decided as the workshop progresses. If you would like to participate, please contact the organisers. This is the second event in the same series, the first one held in June 2026 and can be found here.


Venue: Kalliopi Studios. Closest airport is Chania airport (CHQ).
