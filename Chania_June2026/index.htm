Chania June 2026

Workshop on Model Theory and Applications
Chania, May 31 - June 6, 2026

   

Organizers: Pantelis Eleftheriou (Leeds), Aris Papadopoulos (Maryland)

The goal of this workshop is to bring together researchers with an interest in model theory (and in particular o-minimality and its tame expansions) and/or combinatorics. We plan to run the workshop in an Oberwlfach-style format, where all participants will be considered for giving talks and the program will be decided as the workshop progresses. If you would like to participate, please contact the organisers.



Venue: Kalliopi Studios. Closest airport is Chania airport (CHQ).
