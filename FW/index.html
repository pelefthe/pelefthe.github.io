Final Workshop

Workshop on Tame Geometry and Combinatorics
Leeds, March 16-19, 2026

   

Organizer: Pantelis Eleftheriou (Leeds)

The goal of this workshop is to bring together researchers with an interest in model theory (and in particular o-minimality and its tame expansions) and/or combinatorics. We plan to run the workshop in an Oberwlfach-style format, where all participants will be considered for giving talks and the program will be decided as the workshop progresses. If you would like to participate, please contact p.eleftheriou@leeds.ac.uk. Some funding for junior researchers is available.

The workshop is funded by the EPSRC and the School of Maths of the University of Leeds.

Registration: please send an email with your name, title, affiliation and dates of attendance to p.eleftheriou@leeds.ac.uk by February 15, 2026. For junior researchers, please mention if financial support would be needed.

Venue: University of Leeds, School of Maths, MALL room. Closest airports include Leeds-Bradford (LBA) and Manchester (MAN).
